Counting Cards

Counting Cards is a method used to predict the likelihood of being dealt high-value cards in Blackjack and adjusting your bet accordingly (to increase the chances of winning). When low cards are dealt, the count increases. When high cards are dealt, the count decreases. If the count is high, you should bet higher. If the count is low, you should bet lower.

This website consists of tools to help you practise card counting and begin building that skill. To proceed, select a difficulty level:
