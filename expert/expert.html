Counting Cards - Expert

For this difficulty level, you will play some actual Blackjack! Your goal is to keep count of the cards while also playing the game.

Instructions

Click the deal button to deal a hand of cards. You'll receive your two cards, face up, and the dealer will receive their two cards (one will be hidden). Your goal is to get as close as possible to 21 without going over. You may "hit", to take another card, or "stand", to end your turn and let the dealer play. The dealer will hit if its total is less than 17, otherwise the dealer will stand. For simplicity sake, special rules are not applied - you will not have the option to split, double down, and the dealer will not automatically win on a natural 21. Aces will always be valued at 11.

At any time, you can enter a guess for the current count value, and click the "Guess the Count!" button to validate your guess. Click "Reset" to start the game over.
